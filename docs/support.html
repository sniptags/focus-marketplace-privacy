Frequently Asked Questions

What is Focus Marketplace?

Focus Marketplace helps users browse popular marketplace websites in a more intentional and distraction-free way.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Focus Marketplace does not require its own account system.

Does Focus Marketplace collect my personal information?

No. Focus Marketplace does not collect or store personal information. Preferences are stored locally on your device.

How do I sign in to Facebook Marketplace or other marketplaces?

Sign-in is handled directly by the marketplace provider. Focus Marketplace does not have access to your usernames, passwords, messages, or account data.

What does Greyscale Mode do?

Greyscale Mode reduces visual stimulation by displaying marketplace content in shades of grey, helping users stay focused while browsing.

What are Session Reminders?

Session reminders help you stay aware of your browsing time by displaying periodic notifications based on your selected interval.

Can I change my country later?

Yes. You can reset your country selection at any time from the Settings screen.

Is Focus Marketplace affiliated with Facebook, Meta, Kijiji, Karrot, OLX, or other marketplaces?

No. Focus Marketplace is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any marketplace provider.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Send us an email at focusmarketplacesupport@gmail.com and include as much detail as possible. We appreciate your feedback.
