Frequently Asked Questions

What is Focus Marketplace?

Focus Marketplace helps users browse popular marketplace websites in a more intentional and distraction-free way.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Focus Marketplace does not require its own account system.

Does Focus Marketplace collect my personal information?

No. Focus Marketplace does not collect or store personal information. Preferences are stored locally on your device.

How do I sign in to Facebook Marketplace or other marketplaces?

Sign-in is handled directly by the marketplace provider. Focus Marketplace does not access your usernames, passwords, messages, or account data.

What does Greyscale Mode do?

Greyscale Mode reduces visual stimulation by displaying marketplace content in shades of grey, helping users stay focused while browsing.

What are Session Reminders?

Session reminders help you stay aware of your browsing time by displaying periodic reminders based on your selected interval.

Can I change my country later?

Yes. You can reset your country selection from the Settings screen.

Is Focus Marketplace affiliated with marketplace providers?

No. Focus Marketplace is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Facebook, Meta, Kijiji, Karrot, OLX, Quikr, Craigslist, or any other marketplace provider.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email us at focusmarketplacesupport@gmail.com with details about the issue or suggestion.
