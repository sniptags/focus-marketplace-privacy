Focus Marketplace Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

Thank you for using Focus Marketplace ("the App"). Focus Marketplace is designed to provide users with a distraction-free way to access online marketplaces while promoting intentional browsing and digital wellbeing. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.


Information We Collect

Focus Marketplace does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The App does not require users to create an account and does not maintain a user database. At this time, Focus Marketplace does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Marketplace activity
  • Messages
  • Browsing history outside of the App

Local Device Storage

To provide app functionality, Focus Marketplace may store certain preferences locally on your device. Examples include:

  • Selected country
  • Selected marketplace
  • Session reminder settings
  • Daily browsing limit preferences
  • Focus mode settings
  • Grayscale mode settings
  • Onboarding completion status

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Focus Marketplace or any third party.


Third-Party Marketplace Services

Focus Marketplace provides access to third-party marketplace websites and services, including but not limited to:

  • Facebook Marketplace
  • Kijiji
  • Karrot
  • Craigslist
  • Additional marketplace services that may be added

When you access these services, you interact directly with the respective service provider. Your use of those services is governed by their own:

  • Privacy Policies
  • Terms of Service
  • Cookie Policies
  • Data Processing Practices

Focus Marketplace does not control and is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.


Authentication

Some marketplace services may require users to sign in. When you authenticate with a third-party marketplace:

  • Authentication occurs directly between you and the marketplace provider.
  • Focus Marketplace does not collect or store usernames or passwords.
  • Focus Marketplace does not access authentication tokens.
  • Focus Marketplace does not have access to your messages, purchases, transactions, or account information.

Cookies and Session Data

Third-party marketplace providers may use cookies, local storage, session identifiers, and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain login sessions
  • Remember user preferences
  • Improve security
  • Deliver marketplace functionality
  • Personalize user experiences

These technologies are controlled by the third-party provider and not by Focus Marketplace. Focus Marketplace does not access, modify, collect, sell, or share information contained in such cookies or session data.


Analytics

Focus Marketplace does not currently use analytics services, advertising identifiers, behavioral tracking technologies, or user profiling systems. If analytics functionality is introduced in future versions of the App, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.


Advertising

Focus Marketplace does not currently display third-party advertisements. If advertising is introduced in future versions of the App, this Privacy Policy will be updated before such features become available.


Children's Privacy

Focus Marketplace is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.


Security

Because Focus Marketplace does not collect or store personal information on its servers, the amount of personal data processed by the App is minimal. Users are responsible for protecting their accounts with third-party marketplace providers.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
